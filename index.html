Portal

Controls

  • F: Shot!
  • A: Aim Portal Left
  • W: Aim Portal Up
  • D: Aim Portal Right
  • S: Aim Portal Down
  • Shift: Switch Portal Color
  • Left Arrow: Move Left
  • Up Arrow: Jump
  • Right Arrow:Move Right

Game Tips

  • When a portal-projectile strikes a wall, a portal is opened.

  • Shooting an existing portal with the other color of portal will close both.

  • If both a green and purple portal exist, the player teleports from one to the other upon entering either portal.

  • Falling in lava will result in your death.

  • Good Luck!
